# Grakhs
Grakhs is a Minecraft Bedrock add-on that adds piglin-like monsters to the overworld, appearing during the day and adding an extra layer of danger to your adventure. ⚔️

Grakhs are monsters similar to piglins that spawn in all biomes during the day in the Overworld, in herds of one to three. They carry their own axe, which they do not drop when defeated. They are highly aggressive toward players as well as other monsters, and when defeated by a player, they drop iron nuggets.

❤️ Health: 30 | ⚔️ Attack: 3 | ✨ EXP: 20

This addon is optimized to run effectively on Realms and dedicated servers with minimal hardware resource consumption and its available in english and español.  🇬🇧 🇲🇽
