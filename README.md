# Grakhs
Grakhs is a MCBE Add-on that adds piglin-like monsters to the overworld, appearing during the day and adding an extra layer of danger to your adventure. ⚔️

❤️ Health: 30 | ⚔️ Attack: 3 | ✨ EXP: 20

They spawn in herds of 1 to 3 with 3 different models. 👹 They are highly aggressive, attack both players and monsters, and come equipped with their own axe, which is not dropped upon defeat. 🪓 When killed, they drop iron nuggets. 💰
